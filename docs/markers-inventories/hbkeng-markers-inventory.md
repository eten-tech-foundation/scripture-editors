### Biggest gaps

- **High-volume**: `scr` (231k), `ver` (63k), `scv` (34k), `mod` (31k), `grk`/`heb` transliteration, `res`, `a1`/`a3`/`a2`/`aq1`/`aq2` (alternate translations)
- **Tables**: full family unimplemented — `tr`, `tc1`–`tc4`, `th1`–`th4`, `tcr1`/`tcr2`, `tcc1`–`tcc4`, `thc1`–`thc4`
- **Deeper levels**: `ms4`–`ms7`, `li5`–`li7`, `io5`–`io7`, `ili3`–`ili5`, `q5`/`q6`, `ims`/`ims1`–`ims4`, `ipi1`–`ipi3`
- **Chiasmus**: `ch1`–`ch7` (entirely unimplemented)
- **Other**: `uw`/`+uw` (underline), `tec`, `sec`, `man`, `src`, `ver1`
